## About SANS Cyber Academy

SANS Cyber Academy is a tuition-free, intensive cybersecurity training program designed to prepare students for careers in security operations, incident response, and threat detection. The program includes:

- Hands-on technical training from SANS instructors
- Real-world security operations scenarios
- GIAC certification preparation
- Career placement support

---

## Why I Applied

After building an independent detection engineering portfolio over 6 months, SANS Cyber Academy provides the opportunity to:
- Formalize hands-on skills with industry-recognized SANS/GIAC credentials
- Learn from experienced security practitioners
- Gain exposure to enterprise-scale security operations
- Accelerate career entry into Detection Engineering roles

**SEC504: Hacker Tools, Techniques & Incident Handling**
- Target Certification: GCIH
